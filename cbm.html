The CBM Program

What is a Campus Brand Manager?

LIVE Campus Brand Managers (CBM) are enthusiastic, proactive, and innovative students at universities that are on-campus advocates for LIVE Competition. You will be working alongside other CBMs that are a part of the LIVE Program. You will also be receiving constant support from the LIVE team and will be working collaboratively together to bring forward a successful LIVE Competition 2017. Our External Relations Manager will be your main point of contact throughout your term as a CBM.

Roles and Responsibilities

  • Represent LIVE and serve as a liaison between students from your campus and LIVE
  • Expand LIVE’s brand by working collaboratively with LIVE’s External Relations Manager and fellow CBMs
  • Develop and execute marketing strategies specialized for your campus
  • Educate students about LIVE by addressing and answering questions regarding this year’s competition
  • Promote LIVE Competition to fellow peers through various mediums

What's in it for me?

    Development

    Expand your potential with the CBM Program. You will master relevant skills in communication and leadership in representing a nation wide brand.

    Recognition

    Represent your university at LIVE! LIVE cannot happen without aspiring individuals like you, we want everyone to see all of your hard work on the day of the competition.

    Team

    Together as a team, we collaboratively create a unique competition for the best students across the nation.

    Network

    Expand your professional network! Build relationships with university facilities and with other student leaders just like you.

    Workshops

    Take this opportunity to know everything that goes into creating a national business competition. LIVE wants to provide the best resources for you to stand out from the crowd.

    Experience

    Ability to manage a brand known worldwide to thousands of undergraduate students.
