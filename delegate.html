What Is LIVE?

  • About Us

    LIVE Competition 2017 is Canada’s leading National Multidisciplinary Case Competition co-hosted by Nestlé and the University of Toronto. This year’s competition will welcome 160 of the brightest undergraduate students across North America to participate in our rigorous competition rounds, networking events, and exciting socials over 2 unforgettable days. The winning team will be rewarded with a $1,500 cash prize, various swag items from our partners, and exclusive 1-on-1 mentorships with Senior Corporate Executives from some of the most successful organizations in North America. case competition across North America.

  • Our Competition

    Unique to our competition is our real-time Business Simulation which allows our delegates to compete directly in a Virtual Economy for the chance to capture market share from their competitors and accumulate profits for their virtual company. Furthermore, LIVE delegates will also get to experience multiple areas of business management through our innovative cases, networking events, and influential guest speakers.

Our Title Partner

This year, we take our case competition to the next level through a very special partnership with Nestlé. LIVE Competition and Nestlé executives are collaborating to deliver one of LIVE Competition’s most ambitious case yet! This round will be distributed to our delegates 3-weeks prior to the competition and will surely prove a challenge to all. The deliverable will take the form of a presentation at LIVE Competition and will be judged by Corporate Representatives from Nestlé.
