What Is LIVE?

  • About Us

    LIVE is an annual case competition held in downtown Toronto by the University of Toronto Scarborough, for the brightest undergraduate business students across North America. LIVE is one of Canada's leading multidisciplinary case competitions. Unique to our competition we expose delegates to all areas of business management through our unique case simulations, networking events, and influential guest speakers. Founded in 2006, LIVE will be in its 12th year of operations, with over 150 delegates from over 15 universities, over 50 corporate representatives, LIVE has grown into a well-known prestigious case competition across North America.

    LIVE is an annual case competition held in downtown LIVE's mission is to strive for innovation in the way we deliver an interactive and rewarding competitive experience for our delegates. We strive for development, not only as a competition but to provide experiences and opportunities for personal development.
