What Is LIVE?

  • About Us

    LIVE Competition is a two-day business competition hosted by the University of Toronto. Currently in our 12th year of operations, we welcome 160 of the brightest undergraduate students from across the nation to compete. Our innovative curriculum challenges delegates in an interactive simulated business economy that replicated real life wins and losses of the business world. The curriculum is built from scratch, which allows us to tailor it according to your recruitment needs. The competition also includes networking and social events, as well as guest speakers an awards gala.

  • Our Mission

    LIVE’s mission is to create a platform where our delegates can showcase their diverse skillsets and for our partners to interact with the leaders of tomorrow. We strive to accomplish this mission, through interactive, innovative, and rewarding rounds customised according to our partners’ needs.

Why Get Involved?

  • Curriculum

    Place our delegates to the test on your own terms. You have the chance to shape our competition rounds according to your needs! Our Curriculum Team will ensure that our rounds highlight exactly what you are looking for.

    • Provide key skills that you would like to see in our delegates! Our Curriculum Team can create a round that highlights these skills
    • Provide a general task that our delegates have to complete! This task can be implemented into an innovative round

  • Recruitment

    Get ready to meet some of the brightest students across Canada! Enter the undergraduate network to capitalize on fresh ideas and student talents.

    • Witness our delegates’ capabilities by being a judge during our rounds
    • Network with our delegates at our LIVE Corporate Connections networking event
    • Set up a booth to represent your company
    • Access to resume book filled with resumes of all the delegates

  • Marketing

    Get your brand recognized within the undergraduate community by showing that you are invested in their development and creating these relationships early!

    • Internal and external marketing materials, including digital and print publications
    • Exposure on LIVE’s social media channels
    • Personalized social media contest to increase your company’s exposure
